This is a non-functional demo concept of a blockchain messaging service / cryptocurrency wallet for a fake cryptocoin called STARKcoin. Right now, the front end is in an MVP state, but you can see below that there's an "under the hood" section that allows you to see the responses as the blockchain is activated and verified. Both messages and payments are sent through the blockchain, so you can make sure that both your payments and messages arrive safely and securely.
